Course Overview

This independent study bridges the gap between basic 3D-printed prototypes and manufacturing-ready hardware. Through hands-on experimentation, I am learning to translate a conceptual hardware enclosure into a high-fidelity physical product — applying DFM principles, silicone/resin casting, and desktop injection molding.

Each project in this book documents both the theory and the actual making process, including mistakes, adjustments, and reflections along the way.

Learning Goals

  • Understand and apply core DFM rules in CAD — draft angles, parting lines, wall thickness
  • Design internal structural features: mounting bosses, snap-fits, reinforcing ribs
  • Gain hands-on experience with mold-making, resin casting, and injection molding
